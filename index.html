Memory Test

What to expect

Learn words: You will see words labeled by category (e.g., animal or furniture). Try to remember them.

Memory challenge: A short number-sequence game to reset your short-term memory.

Recall words: You will be asked to recall the words you studied.

Category cues: If needed, we will provide the category as a hint.

Tips

Take your time.
It’s okay if you don’t remember everything.
Try your best.

This test takes about 15–20 minutes.
