Memory Test

What to Expect:

  1. Word Learning: You'll see some words with categories (like 'animal' or 'furniture'). Try to remember them.
  2. Memory Challenge: A fun number sequence game to test your memory.
  3. Remembering Words: We'll ask you to recall the words you saw earlier.
  4. Categories Help: If you can't remember a word, we'll give you the category to help.

Tips:

  • Take your time
  • It's okay if you don't remember everything
  • Try your best

The test will take about 15-20 minutes to complete.
