Memory Test

What to expect

  • Learn words: You will see words grouped by category. Try to remember them.
  • Memory challenge: A short number-sequence game helps reset your short-term memory.
  • Recall words: You will be asked to recall the words you studied.
  • Category cues: If needed, we will provide the category as a hint.

Tips

Take your time. It’s okay if you don’t remember everything—just do your best.

This test takes about 15–20 minutes.
